TEHCLAND is actively pursuing environmental funding programs including Ontario's Species at Risk Stewardship Program, Land Stewardship and Habitat Restoration Program, and other ecosystem revitalization grants.

These grants support the removal of invasive species, enhancement of pollinator habitats, and protection of waterway health — all of which align directly with our restoration goals. Our land qualifies based on ecological value and potential for species recovery, including habitat for monarch butterflies and other at-risk native species.

Why Support Helps

Many of these programs require matched funding, volunteer activity, or in-kind contributions. This is where community matters — your support strengthens our ability to receive government funding and scale our restoration impact.

Active Funding Targets

  • Ontario Species at Risk Stewardship Fund (2025–2026 cycle)
  • Ontario Land Stewardship and Habitat Restoration Program
  • Conservation Land Tax Incentive Program (CLTIP)

Grants fund native seed planting, erosion protection, eco-volunteer programs, signage, and future education hubs. With the river running through our property and natural slope access, we are uniquely positioned for environmental transformation — from day one.
